RAGE BAIT

Rage bait is a slightly more malicious subset of bait specifically aimed at making people angry.

Making strangers angry on the internet is a long standing and rich tradition. It predates social media but has really flourished in the modern age. Like other forms of bait, rage bait attempts to provoke its victims into a response. Not always for the money though, some people post rage bait for the love of the game.
